How to Improve Your Golf Swing — Tips from a PGA Pro in Devon

Updated: April 2026 | Reading time: 5 minutes

Whether you're playing at the Royal Brixham Golf Club or on the links in Torquay, these professional tips will help you break 80 this season. Brought to you by the Cayman Golf community.

Tip #1: Fix Your Grip First

Every swing starts with the grip. 80% of slice problems come from a weak grip position. Place your left hand (for right-handed golfers) so that you can see 2-3 knuckles at address. This promotes a square club face at impact.

Tip #2: The Takeaway Is Everything

Your first 12 inches of backswing set up the entire motion. A common mistake: lifting the club with your hands instead of turning your shoulders. Think "one-piece takeaway" — arms and body move together.

Tip #3: Weight Transfer

At the top of your backswing, 70% of your weight should be on your right foot. Through impact, this shifts to 80% on your left. Practice stepping forward with your left foot to feel this transfer.
