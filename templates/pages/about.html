About Cayman Golf

18 holes of pure golfing heaven nestled in the beautiful Devon countryside on the outskirts of Brixham

A Unique Golfing Experience

At Cayman Golf, we use innovative 'Cayman' golf balls that prioritise precision over distance. This design makes the game more accessible, emphasising skill and accuracy rather than power.

Our course layout promotes a sustainable approach to golf, minimising land use and reducing the need for excessive water, pesticides, and insecticides.

Whether you're a seasoned player looking to refine your accuracy or a family seeking an exciting day out, Cayman Golf is the perfect destination.
