Dan — PGA Qualified Professional

Dan is a fully qualified PGA Professional and the head instructor here at Cayman Golf in the beautiful Torbay area. With over 15 years of experience, he has coached players of all abilities, from juniors taking their first swings to seniors refining their game.

Dan specializes in building solid, repeatable swings tailored to your natural body movement. Whether you want to fix a slice, improve your short game, or prepare for your first competition, Dan provides the expert guidance you need to lower your scores.
